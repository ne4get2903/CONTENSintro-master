New plattform for arts
 Culture lab

CULTURE LAB 2020 offers artists a platform to present their ideas and concepts to the public, live and online. For the artistic collaboration with Germany and Europe, they provide valuable impulses, especially in the areas of: multimedia performance, music and dance. Culture Lab is an initiative by the Goethe-Institut to support independent artists during the Pandemic.
